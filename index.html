Our Concept

Build-A-Beer is a start-up that aims to revolutionize the beer industry by offering a truly customizable beer brewing experience on demand. With its innovative technology, customers can easily choose various attributes of a beer on a slider and within minutes a beer of those specifications is produced. This is the world's first customizable beer experience in a pub setting.
